Getting started

Before you begin

This kit is structured in six steps. Work through them in order for the best results.

Have your Google Admin Console open before starting. You will need it for some questions under Access & Permissions, External Sharing, and Compliance & Risk.

Step 01
Scorecard
Answer 20 questions across 5 governance areas. Each response is worth 1–3 points. Be honest — an accurate baseline is more valuable than a flattering one.
Step 02
Results
See your total score, maturity level, and a breakdown across all five areas. A personalised priority focus is highlighted based on your weakest area.
Step 03
Gap analysis
Log specific gaps, assign a priority (High / Medium / Low), an owner, and a target date for each issue so nothing is missed.
Step 04
Quick wins
10 specific actions you can complete this week, each with the Admin Console path noted. Tick them off as you go.
Step 05
Action plan
Structure your remaining improvements into 30, 60, and 90-day phases with owners and status tracking.
Step 06
Summary export
Your complete results, gaps, and plan in one shareable view — ready to brief your team or leadership.

How scoring works

Each of the 20 questions is worth 1 to 3 points. Score 3 if you have this fully under control, 2 if there is partial progress, and 1 if this area is not addressed. If you are unsure, score 1 and investigate.
