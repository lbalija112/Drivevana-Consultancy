How it works

Clear steps and a fixed scope to avoid time waste.

1) Discovery (30 mins)

Quick call to confirm goals, team setup, and what “good” looks like.

2) Drive Audit (60 mins)

I identify the key issues, risks, and quick wins inside your Drive.

3) Improvements (60 mins)

I implement a clean top-level structure and fix priority permissions.

4) Handover (30 mins)

Walkthrough call + written rules so everyone knows where things are.

What’s not included (so scope stays tidy)
